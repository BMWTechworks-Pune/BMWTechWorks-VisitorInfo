General Evacuation Procedure â€“ Earthquake
When you feel the tremors, protect yourself as quickly as possible. The National Disaster Management Division (NDMD), Ministry of Home Affairs, recommends the following three actions, known as Drop â€“ Cover â€“ Hold:
DROP: Get down on your hands and knees to prevent being knocked over.
COVER: Protect your head and neck with your arms, take shelter under a sturdy table or near an interior wall, and stay away from windows, glass, and heavy furniture.
HOLD: Stay in position and hold onto your shelter until the shaking stops.
If You Get Trapped: Stay calm, cover your nose and mouth to protect yourself from dust, shout, whistle, or tap on nearby objects to attract attention, and remain alert for fallen electrical lines, gas leaks, or unstable structures.
